Legal · Privacy

Privacy Policy

GameDrawer is local-first by design. Your gameplay records stay on your device unless you choose features that move them elsewhere.

Last updated: May 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Lauterstar handles information for GameDrawer ("GameDrawer", "we", "us", or "our"). GameDrawer has no social features, no public profiles, no advertising, and no Lauterstar-owned servers that hold your gameplay data.

Information You Store in GameDrawer

GameDrawer lets you record game sessions, best times, win and loss totals, theme choices, and related settings. By default, this information is stored locally on your device.

We do not collect this gameplay data on Lauterstar servers, sell it, use it for advertising, or use it to track you across apps or websites.

Optional iCloud Sync

If you choose to sign in with Apple, GameDrawer can sync your records through Apple's CloudKit using your private iCloud account, in the iCloud.com.lauterstar.gamekit private container. Apple handles that sync infrastructure under Apple's terms and privacy policies. GameDrawer stores the Apple user identifier needed to keep you signed in and manage sync state.

You can use GameDrawer without signing in. If you sign out, future changes stay on the device. You may also choose whether to erase records stored on the current device.

Stats Export and Import

GameDrawer includes JSON export and import tools so you can back up or move your records. Exported files are controlled by you and may be saved to locations such as Files, iCloud Drive, or another storage provider you choose. The export schema is versioned so older files remain readable.

Diagnostics

GameDrawer's Apple privacy manifest declares no collected data and no tracking. The app uses iOS's standard os.Logger for on-device debugging only — those logs do not leave your device. Apple may provide crash reports or diagnostics if your device settings allow that. Our static website host may process basic technical logs, such as IP address, browser, and request information, to operate and secure the website.

Permissions

GameDrawer does not request access to your camera, microphone, location, contacts, photos, calendar, or health data. The only optional permission is Sign in with Apple for iCloud sync.

Children

GameDrawer is rated 4+ on the App Store. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If a child uses GameDrawer, a parent or guardian should supervise their use of the app and any optional Apple services.

Your Choices

You can keep GameDrawer fully local-only, disable iCloud sync by signing out, export your records, delete individual records in the app, and delete the app to remove its on-device data.
