App Features

01 / Minesweeper

Minesweeper, properly tuned.

Three difficulties: Easy 9×9 (10 mines), Medium 16×16 (40), Hard 16×30 (99). First-tap safety is guaranteed. Flood-fill auto-reveal for empty cells.

02 / Local-first

Your stats stay yours.

Best times, win percentages, and game records live on-device via SwiftData. Survives restarts, fresh installs, and OS updates. No servers we own.

03 / No ads, ever

No coins. No energy. No popups.

Permanent. No banner ads, no forced video ads, no fake currency, no subscription pestering, no required onboarding. Open the app, play.

04 / Themes

34 theme presets.

Six DesignKit families (Classic, Sweet, Bright, Soft, Moody, Loud) plus a custom palette editor. Every game adapts to the look you pick.

05 / Stats that stick

Best times, win %, games played.

Per-difficulty bests, lifetime totals, and a full record table. Export it all as JSON, re-import on a new device, or never look at it again.

06 / Optional iCloud

Sync only if you ask.

Sign in with Apple unlocks CloudKit sync across your devices. Off by default. Sign out and your records stay on the device.
